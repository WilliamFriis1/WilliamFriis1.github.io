About Me

Passionate about game development and fascinated by all things tech, from software engineering to hardware and design. My programming journey began with building simple websites at a young age.

Later, I discovered object-oriented programming through Java in secondary school, which sparked a lasting interest in software design. Transitioning to C# felt natural, and I’ve since spent countless hours developing side projects and exploring advanced programming concepts.

During university, I dove into C++ and gained a deeper understanding of optimization, algorithms, and data structures. Skills I now look to refine even more.

I’m always eager to learn, experiment, and collaborate on projects that push creative and technical boundaries.
